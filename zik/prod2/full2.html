Active Noise Control

To insulate yourself from extraneous noise, Parrot has developed an ultra-high performance active noise control system which eliminates up to 98% of ambient noises.

While traditional ANC audio headsets only use two microphones to analyse ambient noises, the Zik has four microphones located inside and outside each earpiece offering total listening comfort.

Touch control panel

Parrot ZIK is fitted with an ultra-intuitive touch control panel on the right headphone: when connected via Bluetooth, slide your finger vertically to adjust the volume and horizontally to skip to the next track.

Parrot has developed a complete movement recognition algorithm designed to be simple and intuitive for the user.

Touch control panel

Adjust Volume

Sliding your finger vertically on the touch sensitive control panel adjusts the volume of your music or the caller voice.

Touch control panel

Equalizer

The Parametric EQ is a 7 bands equalizer (Center frequencies are: 60 Hz, 150 Hz, 400 Hz, 1 kHz, 2.4 kHz, 6 kHz and 15 kHz).

The user can choose from among the various presets (Flat, Pop, Club, Punchy, Deep, Crystal, User) to adjust the frequencies to the style of music you are listening to.

Touch control panel

Change Track

Sliding your finger horizontally on the touch sensitive control panel plays the next track or return to the previous one.

Touch control panel

SmartPhone Application

Parrot AUDIO SUITE allows the end-user to easily configure and modify the configuration and parameters through the iPhone/iPod/Smartphone user-interface.
